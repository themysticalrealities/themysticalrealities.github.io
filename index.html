The Báb

This is the volume that launched the extraordinary Mystical Realities series. The life of the Báb is explored through carefully researched stories, beautiful Persian music and breathtaking chants. The extraordinary history of the Bábí Faith comes alive in this production. Truly a first of its kind, this best-selling audio recording is now being enjoyed throughout the Bahá'i world.

Stream

The Báb and Quddús

This is the second volume in the Mystical Realities series. It focuses on Quddús, the Last Letter of the Living, and his special station within the small group of Bábís who introduced the new Faith at great peril throughout Persia and Iraq. Perfect for Feasts, Deepenings and Study Groups, this volume contains original Persian music and a number of chanted Writings that will stir your soul.

Stream

The Báb and Mullá Husayn

This fully-scored production is the most ambitious title yet in The Mystical Realities series. Experience the dramatic story of the first Letter of the Living from his studies with Shaykh Ahmad to his martyrdom at the Shrine of Fort Tabarsi. With beautiful Persian chants and select passages from the Writings of the Báb, this volume will inspire you and provide new insights into the heroic age of the Bahá'i Faith.

Stream

The Báb and Táhirih
